What is Preworkout?

Pre workout is a substance, traditonally in a powder form to be dissolved inside of a liquid, that is packed with caffeine. Most gym-goers use pre workout to give them a caffeine rush during their workout and have a better time in the gym with their surge of energy.

The Danger With Preworkout

Preworkout can be dangerous when taken in high dosage, since some of pre workouts will match or go over the recommened amount of caffeine. This causes risk of heart attack and high blood pressure. When taken in moderation, pre workout is something very useful, but gym-goers have to be mindful about how much caffeine they take.
